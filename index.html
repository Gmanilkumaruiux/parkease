About ParkEase

Our mission is to eliminate urban congestion through digital reservations.

ParkEase is a next-generation SaaS parking slot booking framework built for the modern smart city. By linking vehicle telemetry, GPS coordinate mapping, and smart gate cameras, we bridge the gap between drivers and parking branch operators.

Drivers avoid circling lanes, managers automate slot allocation layouts, and cities benefit from reduced emissions and traffic congestion. Currently operating in over 45 major metro centers globally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about space booking protocols.

How do I access the parking lot upon arrival?
Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive a QR code on your dashboard. Simply position this QR code in front of the gate entry camera at the branch, and the barrier gate will lift automatically.
What if I stay past my booked duration?
If you exceed your booked duration, the system logs an overstay warning. The manager dashboard is alerted, and additional fees are charged to your linked payment method upon exit.
Can I cancel my reservation?
Yes! Reservations can be cancelled up to 1 hour before the scheduled start time. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.
